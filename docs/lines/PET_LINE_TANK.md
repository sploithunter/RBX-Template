## Definition

**Front-line wall** — high HP/EHP, soaks hits, holds aggro so squishier pets survive. Grass *owns*
tank (the lean), but a control origin still wants a body, so **Ice fields the recurring polar-bear
wall** too. Tanks frequently **hybridize**: Ice tanks are tank/**control**, Grass apex tanks are
tank/**heal** (or drain in hell) — so this line overlaps Controller and Support a lot.

## Where tanks live

- **🌿 Grass** — the origin's lean. The **Uncommon tank** (the varied stag→rhino→beetle→tortoise
  body) *and* the **Mythic apex** (Ent / Colossus, tank+heal/drain). At **L3 the Grass apex is the
  Verdant/Blight DRAGON** (a tank dragon).
- **❄️ Ice** — the **Rare wall**: the recurring **Bear** (L2+), with **L1 using the Leviathan**
  (epic tank/control) instead. Plus the **Mythic tank/control apex** (Leviathan/Mammoth/Yeti).
- **🏜️ Desert** — **L1 only:** the Sphinx (Gilded/Glass). From L2 Desert goes pure support.

## Designated-power progression

Signature verb = **taunt/pull + mitigation** (thorns / DR aura). Tanks are the role-definers, so they
carry mechanics earlier than melee.

| Slot | Verb | Debut | Escalation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Grass Tank (Stag→Tortoise) | **taunt + thorns** | L3 | L3 thorns → L4 + brief team DR → L5 big thorns + DR aura |
| Ice Wall (Bear) | **taunt/pull + DR aura** | L3 (consider L2) | L3 pull + small DR → L5 DR aura + reflect |
| Grass tank apex (Ent/Colossus) | **thorns + team regen** (tank/heal hybrid) | L2 | scales HP + regen aura; L5 + DR pulse |
| Ice tank/ctrl apex (Leviathan/Mammoth/Yeti) | **stomp slow + DR** (tank/control hybrid) | L1 (light) | slow→bigger AoE slow + DR; see Controller line |

## Overlaps with other lines

- **↔ Controller:** **heavy.** Every Ice tank apex is tank/**control** (the Leviathan/Mammoth/Yeti
  stomp-slows). The Ice Bear wall lives in the *control* origin. Tank and Controller share the Ice egg.
- **↔ Support:** the **Grass tank apex (Ent)** is tank/**heal** (heaven) or tank/**drain** (hell) —
  it's also a support body. The Grass tank dragon (Verdant/Blight, L3) carries a heal/drain aura.
- **↔ Dragon:** the **Grass dragons (L3, Verdant/Blight) are tanks** — the dragon line's tank entry.
- **↔ Melee:** bruisers (Fire Lion, Grass Badger) are the melee/tank blur below the true tanks.
- **Within-line:** Ice Bear vs Grass Tank must stay distinct — Bear = pull+DR (control-flavored wall),
  Grass = thorns+regen (sustain wall). Same role, different *verb*, so they're not redundant.
