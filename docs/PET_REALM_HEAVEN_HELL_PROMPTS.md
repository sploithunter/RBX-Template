[PET_REALM_HEAVEN_HELL_ROSTER.md](PET_REALM_HEAVEN_HELL_ROSTER.md)). Each is wrapped in the
standard frame: starts with *"In the same style with a plain white background (no shadows) give
me a low poly image of"* and ends *"game ready, baked texture."* — paste one at a time.
